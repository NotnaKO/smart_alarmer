Smart Alarmer helps you get out of bed by making you complete a short puzzle sequence before an alarm can be dismissed.

BUILD YOUR WAKE-UP CHALLENGE

Choose from math, typing, memory, and shake puzzles. Select the puzzle types and number of tasks for each alarm.

FLEXIBLE ALARMS

• Create one-time or repeating alarms
• Add labels such as Work, Gym, or Medication
• Choose a custom alarm sound
• Enable gradual volume
• Preview the puzzle sequence safely before relying on it

DESIGNED FOR RELIABLE ALARMS

Smart Alarmer uses Android's alarm-clock capabilities, foreground notifications, and lock-screen alarm display. Enabled alarms are restored after a device restart or time-zone change.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Alarm settings stay on your device. Smart Alarmer has no accounts, ads, analytics, or tracking and does not transmit or share user data.

Important: Android may ask you to allow notifications, alarms and reminders, and full-screen alarm display. These permissions are used only for alarms you create.
