Smart Alarmer Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Smart Alarmer is developed by NotnaKO. This policy explains how the Smart Alarmer Android application handles information.

Information stored on your device

The app stores alarm times, labels, repeat schedules, puzzle settings, and selected ringtone references locally on your device. This information is used only to schedule and operate alarms.

Shake-puzzle motion readings are processed temporarily on the device while that puzzle is active. They are not stored or transmitted.

Collection and sharing

Smart Alarmer does not create user accounts, display ads, use analytics or tracking SDKs, transmit user data, or sell or share personal information.

Device access

The app requests notification, exact-alarm, full-screen-intent, foreground-service, wake-lock, boot, and audio-setting access only to schedule alarms, play alarm audio, wake the screen, display the alarm over the lock screen, restore schedules after a restart, and control alarm volume.

Retention and deletion

Alarm data remains on the device until you delete an alarm, clear the app data, or uninstall the app. The alarm database is excluded from cloud backup and device transfer. Because the app does not operate an account or remote data service, clearing its storage or uninstalling it deletes all app data.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion assistance, contact the developer through the Smart Alarmer issue tracker.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated when the app's behavior changes. The current version is available from the app and its Google Play listing.
